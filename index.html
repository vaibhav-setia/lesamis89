About Les Amis

Right from its inception in 1989, Les Amis came to be known as the epitome of excellence, quality and professionalism. With the aim of scratching the hidden talents of students we organized the event “PANORAMA 1991, the inception of a vision...” This was about the birth of a phenomenon. The phenomenon called the Les Amis that has now snowballed into a quest for redefining excellence and never looked back after flag shipping “PANORAMA” as an event. Les Amis is like a temple of discovery, a place where latent skills of an individual can be identified and honed. It’s a sense of great pride in the professional quality of our work coupled with an uncompromising determination to achieve excellence in everything we undertake make us a forerunner in stretching the limits.

01.

IMAGINARIUM '16

This year we conducted a fiction based event ‘IMAGINARIUM: Festival de Fiction’ at NITTTR, Chandigarh. It witnessed a huge participation of over 200 students from all around the Tri-city. There were students from over 20 schools and 7 colleges. It provided the participants a platform to showcase their talent through a plethora of events. The events included a full day crisis committee ‘International Peace Committee’, two fantasy-based committees, one based on Harry Potter and the other on Game of Thrones, each of which had a highly reputable and experienced executive board. Additional events included a fiction quiz, “You know Nuffin” and a competition that required a blend of art and writing skills, “I AM STAN”. The response from the participants was overwhelmingly positive.

02.

PANORAMA '15

“In celebration of its 25th year, we presented our flagship event, Panorama in a brand new format consisting of three separate fields of competition: Literary, Business and Art. The event was held at Beant Singh Memorial, Sector 42, Chandigarh. Aside from conventional events such as the Lincoln- Douglas Debate, the Business Quiz, Magazine Design and Bookcover. This gave the students a chance to hone their literary skills. Panorama ‘15 also featured unprecedented events such as the Warli Art competition, where in the participants created a magnificent realm of design using the elementary shapes of art, and BidMasters, which introduced to a real-life a stock market scenario”. The event saw an overwhelming participation.

03.

FOURTH FRONT '14

Carrying forward the legacy of 25 years of excellence, this time we planned FOURTH FRONT which was a Model All India Political Party Meet (AIPPM). This event was held at Chandigarh Judicial Academy, Chandigarh where we witnessed the enthusiastic response of students from different schools and colleges off the tricity. The event provided a platform for candidates to showcase their skills based on diplomacy, debate, politics and governance by placing them in the shoes of leading politicians of the country. We were delighted to welcome Dr. Veer Singh, Director Academic Affairs at Chandigarh Judicial Academy, Shashank Sharma - UN Ambassador of Northern India, Ms. Silky Mittal, alumnus IIM Calcutta and Mr. Surjeet Singh, Ex- IAS Officer as our Chair Committee. The event was an exemplary success that went out to be one of its kind and provided a new horizon especially in terms of learning for the organization as well as for the participation.
